⚠ The Problem

Why Indian banks fail
at complaint management

Banks spend crores resolving complaints one by one. Root causes are never fixed. Customers leave silently.

📥
Siloed Channels
Same customer on email, call and Twitter — 3 agents, 3 tickets, no unified view. Customer repeats everything every time.
🔄
Root Causes Never Fixed
500 complaints about the same issue resolved one-by-one. Nobody asks why. Banks fight the fire daily — never extinguish it.
⏰
Missed RBI Deadlines
Under RBI Ombudsman Scheme 2021, complaints escalate in 30 days. Banks track manually, miss the deadline. Fines follow.
🚪
Silent Customer Churn
A customer's 5th complaint gets the same response as the 1st. No churn flag. No priority. They leave silently.
